Project - inverted FACEBOOK

Our Mission

Our project is aiming to create a simplified, inverted version of Facebook games. Where Facebook aims to connect people and then encourage them to play games, we plan to encourage people to play games and then connect with people.

Our Promise

After logging into our site, users will be able to interact with other people who are also logged in from any game or in the home page library with all the games via a chat function. and creating a sense of community amoung users. Games are simple and vary from minesweeper, sudoku, and clicking-based games. We have included a few screenshots of similar games that we have drawn inspiration from to help demonstrate what the site may include.

Our Connection

We want to connect people playing single-player, casual games, by providing them the option to interact with other people who are actively playing on the same site. Some games may allow other players to interact with other players directly.
